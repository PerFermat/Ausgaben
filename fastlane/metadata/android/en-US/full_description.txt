Ausgaben ("expenses" in German) is a mobile companion to the desktop finance software KMyMoney. Record cash expenses, income and transfers on the go and export them into KMyMoney, instead of typing everything in by hand later.

The app works fully offline and without any vendor account: data is exchanged with KMyMoney through a folder you provide yourself (WebDAV, Nextcloud or SMB/Samba). No ads, no tracking.

Features:

* Seamless KMyMoney integration via .kmy files or CSV import
* Sync through a shared WebDAV or SMB folder — your own server, your data
* Split bookings and transfers, categories, and per-account "places" (track where your cash physically is)
* Charts and analysis per account, place or total
* Portfolio import (securities with buys/sells/dividends, latest prices, net worth)
* Fuzzy voice entry with optional location-based payee suggestion
* Multilingual — English and German built in, more languages via upload
* Biometric lock and encrypted credentials

Note on this F-Droid build: it is the phone app without Google Play Services. The optional Wear OS companion (voice entry from a watch) requires the Google Wear Data Layer and is only available from the project's GitHub releases.

Free and open source (GPL-3.0).
